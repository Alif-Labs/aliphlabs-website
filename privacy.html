Consent

By using AliphLabs's site and mobile phone apps including Ababeel, you consent to the collection and use of your location information as described in this policy.

AliphLabs has a secure transmission system of data encryption. Regular updates take place to maintain and improve AliphLabs's data security.

AliphLabs does not charge our subscribers for the use of the website aliphlabs.com, any web applications, nor mobile phone applications for the Google Play Store (Android), or Apple App Store (iOS) application stores.
